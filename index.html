Knowledge Organization, Representation, and Annotation

Knora is a software framework for storing, sharing, and working with primary sources and data in the humanities.



Developed by the Digital Humanities Lab at the University of Basel, and supported by the Swiss Academy of Humanities and Social Sciences.

Knora is free software, released under the GNU Affero General Public License.
